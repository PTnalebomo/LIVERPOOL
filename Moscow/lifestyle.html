The Beatles Museum

The Beatles were a English rock band that made a lot of hit songs around 1960s. To pay tribute to them the Liverpool council decided to build a museum in their honor. The museum attract fans from all over the world.

The Food

The most famous dish around Liverpool is the scouse named after the people of Liverpool(Scousers). It is a hearty meat stew, usually made with mutton or beef as well as vegetables.

Liverpool Cathedral

Liverpool Cathedral church is the largest cathedral church in the world. A lot of catholics around the world come to this church to worship it can accomodate around 2000 people.

FOOTBALL

You cannot talk about Liverpool and not talk about Football. Liverpool is home to two of the oldest English Premier League teams, Liverpool FC and Everton. Liverpool FC having the upper hand when two teams square off in the Merseyside derby derby.
